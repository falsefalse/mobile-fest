Deadmau5 — that’s pronounced “dead mouse” — is the stage name of Toronto house DJ/producer Joel Zimmerman. Voted “Producer of the Year” at the DJ Mag Awards, he was the most awarded artist at the 2008 Beatport Music Awards, received a Juno (Canadian Grammy) and, at the Ibiza 2008 awards, was awarded “Best Electro DJ.” His innovative style (as shown on 2008’s Random Album Title) seamlessly incorporates techno, trance and electro, and his live performances are experiences that have to be seen, heard and felt to be believed — and not just because of the spectacle of his trademark giant “Mau5head” helmet.
